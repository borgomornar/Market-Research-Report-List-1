<p><p>The Textile Fluorine-free Water Repellence Market includes three main types: Weak Cationic, Weakly Anionic, and Non-ionic. Weak Cationic treatments use positively charged agents to bond with negatively charged fibers, enhancing water repellency. Weakly Anionic agents feature a minimal negative charge, providing balanced adherence and durability. Non-ionic treatments lack charge and are versatile, suitable for various textile fibers without altering their inherent properties. Each type caters to different fabric requirements, ensuring effective water repellency while maintaining eco-friendliness.</p></p>
