<p><p>The distillation packings market comprises three main types: metal, plastic, and ceramic packings. Metal packings, often made from stainless steel, offer high durability and enhanced thermal conductivity, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Plastic packings are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, ideal for lower temperature operations. Ceramic packings provide excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for aggressive environments. Each type serves distinct applications in the distillation process, catering to various industry needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
