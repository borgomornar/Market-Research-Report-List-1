<p><p>High-speed sorters are automated systems designed for efficient sorting of items in logistics and distribution centers. The market can be divided into two main types: case sorters and unit sorters. Case sorters handle larger packages or boxes, typically using automated belts or robotics to direct goods to appropriate destinations, enhancing throughput. Unit sorters, on the other hand, focus on individual items or smaller packages, utilizing mechanisms like angled belts or chutes to optimize sorting accuracy and speed for high volumes.</p></p>
