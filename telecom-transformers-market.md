<p><p>The telecom transformers market comprises two main types: three-phase transformers and single-phase transformers. Three-phase transformers are designed for large-scale power distribution, providing efficient energy transfer and balancing loads across multiple phases, making them suitable for substations and industrial applications. In contrast, single-phase transformers are typically used for smaller, localized applications, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for residential and commercial use. Both types play vital roles in efficiently delivering power to telecom infrastructure, ensuring reliable communication services.</p></p>
