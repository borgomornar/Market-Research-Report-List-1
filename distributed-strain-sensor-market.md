<p><strong>The Distributed Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on the Rayleigh Backscatter Deliver</li><li>Based on Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) Deliver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Strain Sensor market can be categorized based on two primary delivery mechanisms. Firstly, Rayleigh Backscatter relies on the scattering of light within optical fibers to measure strain, enabling continuous monitoring over long distances. Secondly, Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) use periodic variations in refractive index along the fiber to reflect specific wavelengths, providing localized strain measurements at multiple points. Both types effectively cater to diverse applications such as structural health monitoring, civil engineering, and aerospace systems.</p></p>
