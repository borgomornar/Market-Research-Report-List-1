<p><p>The spherical silica gel market is categorized into various types, primarily C18, C8, and others. C18 silica gel, known for its long-chain hydrophobic properties, is widely used in liquid chromatography for retaining non-polar compounds. C8 silica gel is shorter than C18, offering moderate hydrophobicity, suitable for separating a broader range of polarities. Other types may include specialized formulations tailored for specific applications in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and environmental monitoring, enhancing their versatility and function in various analytical processes.</p></p>
