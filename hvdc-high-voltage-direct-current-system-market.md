<p><ul><li>Two-terminal HVDC System</li><li>Multi-terminal HVDC System (MTDC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC market comprises two main types: Two-terminal HVDC systems and Multi-terminal HVDC (MTDC) systems. Two-terminal HVDC systems connect two power sources or grids, enabling efficient long-distance electricity transmission with lower losses. In contrast, MTDC systems facilitate connections among multiple grids or sources, allowing for enhanced flexibility, grid stability, and integration of renewable energy sources. Both systems are crucial for modern power networks, addressing the increasing demand for efficient energy transmission and improving overall grid reliability.</p></p>
