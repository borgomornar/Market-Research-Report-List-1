<p><ul><li>HEPA Technology</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitators Technology</li><li>Ionizers And Ozone Generators Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial smart air purifier market features several key technologies. HEPA technology utilizes high-efficiency particulate air filters to capture allergens and pollutants, ensuring significant particle removal. Electrostatic precipitators use charged plates to attract and trap airborne particles, requiring less frequent filter replacements. Ionizers generate negatively charged ions to clump dust and allergens, enhancing air quality, while ozone generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and pathogens. Each technology offers unique benefits, catering to diverse commercial air purification needs.</p></p>
