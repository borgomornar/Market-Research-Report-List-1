<p><p>Worm gearing encompasses three primary types: single envelope, double envelope, and non-enveloping worm gears. Single envelope worm gears feature a single thread, providing a compact design suitable for low-speed applications. Double envelope worm gears offer enhanced efficiency and smoother operation due to their additional contact surfaces, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Non-enveloping worm gears, characterized by their unique design, allow for higher efficiency and reduced friction, often used in specialized machinery where minimal backlash is critical.</p></p>
