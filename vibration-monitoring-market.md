<p><p>The vibration monitoring market is divided into two main types: online and offline monitoring. Online vibration monitoring involves continuous data collection using installed sensors that transmit real-time information, allowing for immediate analysis and response to operational issues. In contrast, offline vibration monitoring requires periodic manual testing, where equipment is assessed at designated times using portable devices. Both methods are essential for maintaining machinery health, minimizing downtime, and preventing failures in various industries, including manufacturing and energy.</p></p>
