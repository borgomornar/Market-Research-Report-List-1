<p><p>Arylene Methane Dyes comprise two main types: Diarylmethane Dyes and Triarylmethane Dyes. Diarylmethane Dyes are characterized by two aromatic groups bonded to a methane bridge, offering vibrant colors and excellent stability, making them suitable for textiles and plastics. Triarylmethane Dyes feature three aromatic groups, providing even richer hues and higher lightfastness, widely used in inks and coatings. Both types are popular in various applications due to their vivid coloration, versatility, and chemical properties.</p></p>
