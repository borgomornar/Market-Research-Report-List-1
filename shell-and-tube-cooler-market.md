<p><p>A shell and tube cooler is a type of heat exchanger used to transfer heat between two fluids. This equipment consists of a series of tubes, one set located inside a shell, where one fluid flows through the tubes and another fluid flows around them in the shell, allowing for efficient heat exchange. They are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, power generation, and chemical processing due to their robust design, high efficiency, and ability to handle high pressures and temperatures.</p><p>The Shell and Tube Cooler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient cooling solutions. Technological advancements, including the integration of IoT in monitoring systems, are also set to enhance the efficiency and reliability of shell and tube coolers, making them a preferred choice across various applications.</p></p>
