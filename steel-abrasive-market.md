<p><strong>The Steel Abrasive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Shot</li><li>Steel Grit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel abrasive market primarily includes two types: steel shot and steel grit. Steel shot consists of spherical particles, ideal for achieving a smooth surface finish and commonly used in shot blasting applications. It offers durability and consistent performance. In contrast, steel grit features angular particles, which provide aggressive cutting action, making it suitable for rougher surface preparation, such as removing rust or paint. Both types are essential in various industries for surface treatment, cleaning, and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
