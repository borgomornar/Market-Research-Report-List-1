<p><p>The security turnstile market features two primary types: optical turnstiles and arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles use advanced sensors and infrared beams to detect unauthorized access, allowing seamless entry while maintaining high security. They are ideal for environments requiring aesthetic appeal and quick passage, such as corporate offices. In contrast, arm turnstiles incorporate physical barriers that restrict entry until proper credentials are presented. These are commonly used in high-security areas, providing robust control over access points. Both types enhance overall security measures.</p></p>
