<p><p>The Medium and Low Density Fiber Cement Board market encompasses various product types based on thickness. Ultra-thin boards, typically less than 6mm, are lightweight and suitable for interior applications. Conventional boards, ranging from 6mm to 12mm, serve diverse construction needs, combining durability and flexibility. Thick boards, usually between 12mm to 18mm, provide enhanced structural support for external applications. Ultra-thick boards, exceeding 18mm, offer superior strength and are ideal for heavy-duty settings, making them essential in industrial and commercial projects.</p></p>
