<p><ul><li>High Porosity</li><li>Low Porosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The White Glass market is categorized into two distinct types based on porosity: high porosity and low porosity. High porosity white glass features numerous small pores, enhancing its ability to absorb moisture and chemicals, making it suitable for applications requiring breathability, such as in medical and filtration devices. In contrast, low porosity white glass has fewer pores, providing a denser structure that offers better barrier properties and durability, making it ideal for architectural and decorative uses. Each type serves specialized needs across various industries.</p></p>
